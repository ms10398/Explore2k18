JAM

Thought you were good enough at extempore?
Here is where they prove you wrong.
JAM is the teeth grinding, tongue twisting and wit testing event of public speaking.
A game of laughs and a game where one can shine with not his/her teeth but his/her tongue!

Minute to Win it

1. Cookie Face - Each player places a cookie on their forehead, and the goal is to get the cookie into your mouth using only your facial muscles in under 1 minute. No hands! This is tougher than it looks!
2. Stack It Up- Players must stack 25 pennies in under a minute using only one hand.
3. Pong Tac Toe - Each player had one minute to bounce ping pong balls down the length of the table and into their tray – 3 in a row (in any direction) wins!
4. Stack Attack- Players have to stack up a pile of cups and then place them back into a single stack in under a minute.
5. Movin on Up-Each player starts with a stack of 25 red cups with one blue cup at the top of the stack.
Start by moving the top blue cup to the bottom of the stack, and continue moving cups from the top to the bottom until the blue cup is back at the bottom of the stack again.
6. NutStacker-Thread five hex nuts onto a wooden skewer. Using only one hand on the skewer, stack up all of the nuts in under one minute.
7. In this game, players had to bounce ping pong balls into six cups.
8. Player must blow off 52 cards, leaving 1 on top of a glass bottle.
9. Catch 5 balls in a bucket on top of the head
10. Back Flip- keep pensils on back of your hand and then throw it to grab it from the front of your hand.

IPL Auction & Sports Quiz

How many of you watched on enviously as who’s who of the country bid gazillions for the IPL teams and built a team from scratch,
thinking you could do better? How many of you wanted to play Murli with bhajji, Rohit with Sehwag, or felt engaged at Ganguly’s exclusion, but lacked the billions needed?
Well then don’t forget to take part in this unique event combining passion of cricket with the guile of business. This event would be held over 3 rounds on 3 days of PECFEST.
PRELIMS:
This is a Quizzing round based on cricket. Nine teams would be short listed for the final round.
FINALS:
Nine teams would be competing in this round.
Each team would be allotted an IPL team at random and then they have to buy players in the auction to build a complete team.
Each Player has a certain number of points based on brand image,
value, career performance etc. The aim is to collect maximum points in order
RULES:
Prelims:
A Quiz would be held on day one and 9 teams would be short listed from the round 1. The winning teams would be at random allotted an IPL Team and the auction would begin on second day and continue till day 3.
Finals:
A participating team consists of 2-3 players. At first, the team would be auctioned. Along with the team, the participating franchise will be given their respective team of IPL season 2012.
A franchise then can retain at most of 4 players and have to participate in the auction for completion of their team which consists 15 members.
Then in the next step, the batsmen will be auctioned.
A team can have a maximum of 6 overseas players.
Then bowlers will be auctioned and subsequently then wicketkeepers. The nationality of the wicketkeeper is irrespective.
Then all - rounders will be auctioned. The auction will then go through round 2.
Any team which loses all its money before the completion of its auction can sell back the any of the existing players from its team to the auctioner on a price 85% of the highest bid on the player.
The team having maximum points would win the auction.
The base prices would be provided to the team before the start of the auction. The increment in the bid should be in the multiples of 50, 00,000.
Any money left after the completion of the game will be considered if two teams have equal points. If still a tie persists, a question will be asked related to the cricket players.
The franchises can request to bring back any unsold players for auction at the end of round
2. The base prices of the players will remain the same.
A team should have 1 wicket keeper, 3 all rounders, 6 batsmen and 5 bowlers.
